The Vibe: Our Mission

Our mission is to create the most exciting and flavorful snack experience in India, where every pack is a celebration of chaotic creativity, bold taste, and unapologetic individuality. We're not just a snack; we're a statement.

Unforgettable Taste

We take authentic Indian flavors, remix them, and intensify them for a modern palate. This isn't your parent's popcorn. It's a flavor explosion designed to be as bold as you are.

Unmatched Aesthetics

Our "doodle-bomb" packaging is vibrant, chaotic, and inherently shareable. It's designed to be a piece of social currency, turning the product itself into a statement of identity.
